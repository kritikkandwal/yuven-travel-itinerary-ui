Package Overview

Embark on a spiritually enriching & nature-filled 8-day family journey with Yuven Travel, blending divine blessings, mystical energy, and misty mountain charm.

Your trip begins in the sacred town of Tirupati, where you'll seek blessings at the revered Tirupati Balaji Temple, one of India's most important pilgrimage destinations. With an overnight stay, you'll have time to soak in the spiritual ambiance and witness the devotion that fills the air.

Continue to Coimbatore, where you'll visit the iconic Adiyogi Shiva Statue at the Isha Foundation—a monumental figure symbolizing inner transformation and peace. Let the spiritual energy of this powerful site set the tone for the next leg of your journey.

From here, ascend to the tranquil hills of Coorg, fondly known as the "Scotland of India." Over four rejuvenating nights, unwind amidst lush coffee plantations, waterfalls, and rolling greenery. Explore ancient temples, wildlife sanctuaries, and scenic viewpoints while enjoying local cuisine and cool mountain air.

Whether you seek spiritual renewal, quality family time, or peaceful nature retreats, this thoughtfully crafted itinerary offers it all—making it a perfect balance of divinity, discovery, and downtime.

Detailed Itinerary

DAY 1: Arrival in Tirupati – Welcome & Optional Sightseeing

Welcome to the holy city of Tirupati! Upon arrival at Tirupati Airport around noon, our team will receive you with warm hospitality. Enjoy a smooth private transfer to your 5-star hotel. After check-in, take the afternoon to relax and acclimate. If time permits, opt for a serene visit to Padmavathi Temple or the sacred Kapila Theertham. This day is perfect for easing into your spiritual journey. Overnight stay in Tirupati.

Quick Overview:

  • Arrival: Meet & greet at Tirupati Airport around noon.
  • Private transfer to your luxurious 5-star hotel in Tirupati.
  • Check-in and relax to recover from your journey.
  • Optional (based on time): Visit Padmavathi Temple or Kapila Theertham, both serene and spiritually significant.
  • Overnight stay in Tirupati.

DAY 2: Tirupati Balaji Darshan + Transfer to Coimbatore via Chennai

Start your day early for an exclusive VIP Darshan of Lord Venkateswara at the iconic Tirumala Temple. After a divine experience, return to your hotel, freshen up, and check out. You will then be transferred to Chennai Airport for your flight to Coimbatore (not included). On arrival, you'll be greeted and escorted to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day relaxing and rejuvenating after your spiritual morning and travel. Overnight stay in Coimbatore.

Quick Overview:

  • Early morning VIP Darshan of Lord Venkateswara at Tirumala Temple (VIP Pass charges extra).
  • Return to the hotel, freshen up, and complete check-out.
  • Transfer to Chennai Airport for flight to Coimbatore (flight not included).
  • Arrival in Coimbatore by noon; warm welcome by our representative.
  • Private transfer to a 5-star hotel in Coimbatore; check-in and relax for the day.
  • Overnight stay in Coimbatore.

DAY 3: Trip to Isha Yoga Center & Adiyogi Shiva Experience

Today is devoted to spiritual awakening and inner peace. Visit the Isha Yoga Center, set against the serene backdrop of the Velliangiri Hills. Marvel at the majestic 112-ft Adiyogi Shiva Statue, a globally recognized symbol of spiritual energy. Participate in optional meditation or wellness sessions (advance registration may be required). Return to your hotel for a restful evening. Overnight stay in Coimbatore.

Quick Overview:

  • Visit the Isha Yoga Center, nestled at the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains.
  • Witness the awe-inspiring 112-ft Adiyogi Shiva Statue—a UNESCO-recognized symbol of inner transformation.
  • Optional: Participate in guided meditation sessions or wellness programs (subject to availability).
  • Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Coimbatore.

DAY 4: Travel from Coimbatore to Coorg & Enjoy the Scenic Drive

Enjoy a hearty breakfast before embarking on a scenic drive through the lush Western Ghats to reach Coorg. The journey itself is a treat, filled with misty hills, dense forests, and vibrant landscapes. On arrival, check into your 5-star resort and unwind amidst nature. This day is designed for rest, scenic appreciation, and embracing the tranquil vibes of Coorg. Overnight stay in Coorg.

Quick Overview:

  • After breakfast, check-out and begin your journey to Coorg (approx. 6.5–7 hours).
  • En route, enjoy stunning views of Western Ghats, plantations, and forested landscapes.
  • Check-in at a 5-star resort in Coorg and unwind for the evening.
  • Overnight stay in Coorg.

DAY 5: Day Trip to Coorg Sightseeing – Nature & Heritage

Post breakfast, head out for an enriching tour of Coorg's finest attractions. Visit Abbey Falls, a spectacular waterfall surrounded by coffee plantations. Enjoy panoramic views at Raja's Seat and explore the historical Madikeri Fort. Capture the essence of Coorg's culture and scenic charm. Return to your resort for a peaceful evening. Overnight stay in Coorg.

Quick Overview:

  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Visit the scenic Abbey Falls, followed by a panoramic view from Raja's Seat.
  • Explore the historic Madikeri Fort, blending colonial architecture and regional history.
  • Evening at leisure or enjoy resort amenities.
  • Overnight stay in Coorg.

DAY 6: Wildlife & Culture – Dubare & Bylakuppe

Today, experience wildlife and cultural diversity. Visit Dubare Elephant Camp, where you can interact with elephants and learn about their care. Later, journey to Bylakuppe to explore the vibrant Tibetan settlement and its stunning Golden Temple. This blend of nature and spiritual culture offers a unique experience. Overnight stay in Coorg.

Quick Overview:

  • Early breakfast followed by a visit to Dubare Elephant Camp – interact with elephants in a natural setting.
  • Head to Bylakuppe to explore the Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple), a serene Tibetan settlement.
  • Return to the hotel for a relaxed evening.
  • Overnight stay in Coorg.

DAY 7: Coffee Trails & Leisure

Start the morning with a guided plantation walk through the coffee estates of Coorg, discovering the secrets of spice and coffee cultivation. The rest of the day is at your leisure. You may indulge in spa therapies, enjoy the hotel amenities, or simply relax amidst the soothing surroundings. Overnight stay in Coorg.

Quick Overview:

  • Start the day with a guided plantation walk through Coorg's lush coffee estates.
  • Learn about coffee cultivation, spices, and the history of Coorg's agricultural richness.
  • Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure—resort spa, high tea with a view, or family time by the pool.
  • Overnight stay in Coorg.

DAY 8: End of the Trip & Departure for Mysore Airport

This is the end of your trip to South India. Enjoy your breakfast at the hotel, and spend some time with friends & family. Pack your bags for your return journey home. After check-out formalities (The standard check-out time is 12:00 noon), check all your belongings before you leave the hotel. Our driver will drop you at the airport according to your flight timings or as per your convenience. Hope you have enjoyed this customized trip and captured some wonderful lifelong memories. We at Yuven Travel will be glad to cater to all your future travel plans around the world.
